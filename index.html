Quick Safety Tips!

  • Be Alert, Scan the area as you walk.Be aware of your surroundings and walk with confidence.
  • Trust your instincts and avoid situations that feel unsafe.
  • Keep your phone charged and have emergency contacts easily accessible.
  • Learn self-defense techniques.
  • Use safety apps to stay connected with friends and family.
  • Carry your purse close to your body and do not leave it unattended.
